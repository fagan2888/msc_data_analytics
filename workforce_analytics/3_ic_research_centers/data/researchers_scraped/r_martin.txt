//Summary                                                                                                                                                                 Summary                                Ralf Martin joined Imperial College Business School in September 2011. In his research he examines how government policies affect business performance. He is particularly focussing on Climate Change policies, to understand which policies are most effective and efficient in reducing greenhouse gas emission and what effect these policies have on other
