//Summary                                                                                                                                                                 Summary                                Diane Morgan is the Associate Dean of Programmes at Imperial College Business School. Diane leads the vision and strategy for the suite of MBA and MSc programmes and is the champion of the School's teaching and delivery.Diane was formerly Associate Dean of Degree Programmes and Career Services at London Business School and prior to that she was at USC Marshall School of Business and New York University Stern School of Business.An MBA graduate of Columbia Business School, Diane has lived and worked in the US, the UK, Italy, China and the Middle East. She started her career at the Council on International Educational Exchange (CIEE) before joining The New York Times and The Washington Post.Passionate about education and knowledge opening opportunities for both individuals and firms, Diane is on the Board of the Forte Foundation and the European Business Schools/Women on Boards Taskforce.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              //Links                                                                                                                                                                                                                  College Directory                                                                                                                 Search College Directory                                                                                                                Business School                                                                                                                Expert Directory                                                                                                                                                                                                                             //News
