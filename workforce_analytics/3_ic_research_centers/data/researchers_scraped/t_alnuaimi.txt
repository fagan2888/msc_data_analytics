//Summary                                                                                                                                                                 Summary                                Tufool is an Assistant Professor of Data Science and Innovation.Tufool joined  Imperial College Business School in October 2008 as a PhD student. During her PhD, she researched knowledge creation and technological innovation in MNCs, with a particular focus on emerging market economies. The research involved delineating some of the factors that distinguish emerging economy innovation from those in more advanced economies. Tufool’s most recent research endeavours involve stitching and analysing data from disparate sources to examine pertinent questions about innovation and organisational performance.Before joining Imperial, Tufool received her Bachelor's and Master's degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from MIT.
