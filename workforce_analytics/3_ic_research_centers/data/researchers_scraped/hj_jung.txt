//Summary                                                                                                                                                                 Summary                                HeeJung Jung is an Assistant Professor at Imperial College Business School. HeeJung holds a Ph.D. in Management from INSEAD (2014) and a B.A. in Communications (Advertising and Public Relations) from Ewha Woman's University.HeeJung's research involves the study of how founding conditions shape ventures’ performance, future strategies and search behavior with a particular focus on founding team dynamics and learning at founding. In particular, her work uses micro methodological tools to confront macro theories. HeeJung teaches Entrepreneurship, Business Plan Competition, and also acts as jury member in several launchpads in London.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              //Links                                                                                                                                                                                                                  College Directory                                                                                                                 Search College Directory                                                                                                                Business School                                                                                                                Expert Directory                                                                                                                                                                                                                             //News
