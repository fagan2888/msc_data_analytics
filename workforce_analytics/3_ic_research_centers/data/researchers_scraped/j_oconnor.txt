//Summary                                                                                                                                                                 Summary                                Jenny O'Connor is a Research Associate in the Innovation and Enterprise Department at Imperial Business School. She is a social scientist, and is involved in the User Engagement and Technology Diffusion work package of the interdisciplinary Digital City Exchange (DCE) project. This work package covers four core competencies: exploring smart cities -- concept and perceptions; analysing user engagement and diffusion constraints; developing exploratory case studies, analysing stratregic frameworks, and benchmarking potential incentive mechanisms; and evaluating attitudes towards data security, trust and privacyBefore joining Imperial College London, Jenny completed her ESRC-funded PhD at King's College London; this explored functional understandings of institutional trust witin the healthcare sector. She also worked as a software developer for two years in Dublin before completing a MRes in Environment and Development at King's College London. She has a Bachelor of Civil Law and a Higher Diploma in Computer Science from University College Cork in Ireland. Jenny has long standing research interests in organisational behaviour and the role of institutions in contemporary society, particularly in relation to institutional trust, social capital and community.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               //Research Groups                                                                                                                                                                                     Digital City Exchange                                                                                                                                                                                            //Links                                                                                                                                                                                                                  College Directory                                                                                                                 Search College Directory                                                                                                                Business School                                                                                                                Expert Directory                                                                                                                                                                                                                             //News
