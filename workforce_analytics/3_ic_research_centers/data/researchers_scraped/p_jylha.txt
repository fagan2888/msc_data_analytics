//Summary                                                                                                                                                                 Summary                                Petri Jylha joined the Finance Group of the Imperial College Business School in 2012. He holds an MSc in economics from Helsinki School of Economics and a PhD in finance from Aalto University. Petri’s research interests are focused around asset pricing, market imperfections, and investment funds—especially hedge funds and international finance. His work has been published in the Journal of Financial Economics. Petri teaches in the MSc Finance and MSc Risk Management and Financial Engineering Programs. Before pursuing his PhD, Petri worked for three years in the asset management industry.  LinksCVPersonal websiteSSRN author pageLinkedin profile
