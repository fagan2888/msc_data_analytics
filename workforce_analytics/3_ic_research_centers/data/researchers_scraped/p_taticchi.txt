//Summary                                                                                                                                                                 Summary                                Director of the MSc Management, Director of Global Initiatives and Principal Teaching Fellow in Management and Sustainability.Beside his roles at IB, in the recent years Paolo has been visiting New York University where he still has an Adjunct Professor position.Paolo performs scientific research in performance measurement and management, business networks and corporate sustainability. These research activities have been documented in many books, chapters, journal and conference papers. He is the Editor and Co-Author of the books “Business Performance Measurement and Management: new contexts, themes and challenges” and “Corporate Sustainability” published by Springer internationally.Paolo teaches management courses at both the undergraduate and graduate level, and he's a regular speaker in international MBA and EMBA programs in Europe, Africa, Asia, and the Americas.Furthermore, he has significant consultancy experience and entrepreneurial experience. In fact, he co-founded a few companies in different industries.Paolo holds a Bachelor and Master in Mechanical Engineering, a Master in Business Administration with a focus on Innovation and a PhD in Industrial Engineering with a focus Operations Management. LinkedIn: http://uk.linkedin.com/pub/paolo-taticchi/15/552/229Twitter: https://twitter.com/taticchipaolo
